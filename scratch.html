More About Kate

I love running

Running provides an opportunity for me to put everything in my life aside and challenge myself. I played soccer all throughout high school which got me into running. My freshman year I decided to run track and ran Varsity for the 100, 200, and 400 meter. My senior year (2024) I decided to run the Saint George Marathon with my mom.

I love skiing

When I moved to Utah right before high school, my dad promised we were going to become a skiing family so that we wouldn't hate the snow. I love the feeling of being outside and taking something, the snow, which is really easy to be annoyed by, and instead changing that emotion into excitement. Skiing challenges me to face my fears and helps me appreciate nature.

I'm the oldest of 4 kids

Being the oldest of 4 kids has taught me a lot about responsibility and leadership. I have learned to be patient, understanding, and supportive of my younger siblings. I enjoy spending time with them and helping them grow into their own unique individuals.

I lived in the Bay Area

I was born and raised in the Bay Area until I was 14 years old. Living in such a diverse and vibrant area exposed me to different cultures, ideas, and perspectives. I developed a love for learning about new things and meeting different people. The Bay Area also instilled in me a strong work ethic and a drive to succeed.
